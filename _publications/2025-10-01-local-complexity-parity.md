This work studies locally checkable proof (LCP) schemes for certifying parity-type properties in distributed networks, part of a broader line of research on the local complexity of distributed certification.

Joint work with Nicolas Bousquet, Laurent Feuilloley, and Sébastien Zeitoun, arising from a research visit to LIRIS, CNRS, Lyon.
